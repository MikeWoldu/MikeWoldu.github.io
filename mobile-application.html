Mobile Application

Section Header 1

First you start by pressing on the ”SCAN” button [1] on the toolbar to begin searching for available BLE devices

Section Header 2

Then you can either wait until the scanning process is complete or if you see the desired device you can press on the “STOP” button [1] on the tool bar. To select the desired device you just have to click on the name from the list [2]

Section Header 3

After the desired device is chosen, you will be taken to the next page and you will get a “CONNECTING…” text while the BLE device connects to the mobile phone. Once a connection is established you will see a “CONNECTED” message [1]. To start the receiving sensor data from the BLE device, all you have to do is press the “START” button [2]. To measure how long you are received data you can see a timer new to the start button [3] that shows time in minutes and seconds. An identifier is needed to distinguish between the 6 different punching techniques. While receiving data, in order to add an identifier to the incoming data, any one of the 6 button can be pressed as desired [4].

Section Header 4

The incoming data can be seen in real-time at the top of the screen [1]. To keep track of which identifier, i.e. punching technique is chosen, a sequence of number can be seen [2]. The identifier numbers associated to each button are as follows:- JAB = 1 CROSS = 2 LEFT HOOK = 3 RIGHT HOOK = 4 LEFT UPPERCUT = 5 RIGHT UPPERCUT = 6 To stop the session you simply press the “STOP” button [3] after the desired time has passed.
